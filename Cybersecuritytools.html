Some Tools to protect ourselves

Have I Been Pwned

This website allows you to enter your email address and check if it has been involved in any known data breaches. If it has, it will give you information about the breach and what data was exposed.

Check Now

Firefox monitor

Similar to Have I Been Pwned, this website allows you to enter your email address and check if it has been involved in any known data breaches. It also offers tips on how to protect your online accounts.

Check Now

Google Safety Center

Google's Safety Center provides information and tips on how to stay safe online. It covers topics such as protecting your data, avoiding scams, and staying safe on social media.

Check Now

SSL labs

This website allows you to test the security of any website by checking its SSL/TLS certificate. It will provide you with a rating and detailed information on any security issues found.

Check Now

CISA

This is the official website of the U.S. government agency responsible for cybersecurity. It provides a wealth of information on cybersecurity threats, best practices, and resources for individuals and organizations.
