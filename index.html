Cyber Crimes

New technologies create new criminal opportunities but few new types of crime.

Most cybercrime is an attack on information about individuals, corporations, or governments. Although the attacks do not take place on a physical body, they do take place on the personal or corporate virtual body, which is the set of informational attributes that define people and institutions on the Internet.

Types Of Cyber Crimes

Phishing

Phishing frequently poses as a request for information from a reputable third party. Phishing emails invite users to click on a link and enter their personal information.

Read more ->

DDoS Attack

As the name suggests, a denial-of-service (DoS) attack focuses on disrupting network service. Attackers transmit a large amount of data traffic via the network until it becomes overloaded and stops working.

Read more ->

Man-in-the-middle Attack

If the user is banking online, the man in the middle would communicate with the user by impersonating the bank. The man in the middle would receive all information transferred between the user and the bank including sensitive data related to bank accounts.
